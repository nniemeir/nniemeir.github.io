Date: 2026-04-26

Rationale

Like many media streaming platforms, Tubi has a massive library of media that can be difficult to find your way through. Tubi in particular maintains a number of category pages that make it easier to find films and series that you will enjoy, but they only make the full list accessibile on the Television version of their user interface. Since I like the platform overall, I decided to round up links to all of the categories that I am aware of.
